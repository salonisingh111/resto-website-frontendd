Our Story

Redefining Fine Dining

Founded in 2026, Savora began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to create an atmosphere where culinary art meets modern luxury. The dark, intimate aesthetic is designed to strip away distractions, focusing your senses entirely on the flavors, textures, and aromas of our meticulously crafted dishes.

Our Head Chef, Alexandre Dubois, brings over 20 years of Michelin-starred experience, sourcing the finest ingredients globally to craft seasonal menus that inspire and delight.
